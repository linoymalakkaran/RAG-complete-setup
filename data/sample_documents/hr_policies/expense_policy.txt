# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Purpose

This policy outlines the procedures for submitting and approving employee expense reimbursements.

## Eligible Expenses

### Travel Expenses
- Airfare (economy class)
- Hotel accommodations (up to $200/night)
- Ground transportation (taxis, rideshare, rental cars)
- Mileage reimbursement: $0.65 per mile

### Meal Expenses
- Breakfast: Up to $15
- Lunch: Up to $25
- Dinner: Up to $50
- *Note: Alcohol is not reimbursable*

### Business Expenses
- Office supplies
- Client entertainment (requires manager pre-approval)
- Professional development (conferences, courses)
- Technology purchases over $500 require IT approval

## Submission Process

1. **Submit within 30 days** of expense date
2. Attach all original receipts (required for expenses over $25)
3. Use the expense report form in the HR portal
4. Provide business justification for each expense
5. Manager approval required before submission to Finance

## Reimbursement Timeline

- Approved expenses processed **within 2 weeks**
- Direct deposit to employee bank account
- Questions? Contact finance@company.com

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

- Personal expenses
- Alcoholic beverages
- Entertainment without business purpose
- Traffic violations and parking tickets
- First-class travel (unless pre-approved)

*Policy effective: January 1, 2024*
